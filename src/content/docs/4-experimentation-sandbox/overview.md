# Experimentation Sandbox Overview

A safe space to experiment with different AI technologies, test new approaches, and learn by doing.

## What You'll Find Here

- **Jupyter Notebooks**: Interactive tutorials and experiments
- **Sample Code**: Quick proof-of-concept implementations
- **Integration Guides**: How to integrate different Azure AI services
- **Performance Benchmarks**: Compare different models and approaches

## Experiment Categories

- **Model Evaluation**: Test and compare different AI models
- **Prompt Engineering**: Experiment with prompts and parameters
- **Data Processing**: Test data transformation pipelines
- **API Integration**: Explore Azure AI service APIs

## Getting Started

The sandbox provides pre-configured environments for experimentation without affecting production resources.

## Best Practices

- Document your experiments
- Version control your notebooks
- Track costs in sandbox environments
- Share learnings with the team
